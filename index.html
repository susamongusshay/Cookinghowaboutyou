Our Story

Born from a love of real cooking

CookingHowAboutYou is a YouTube cooking channel run by three friends who believe the best meals are the ones you make yourself. No fancy equipment, no culinary school — just good food and genuine fun.

From crispy skillet pizzas to silky Fettuccine Alfredo, Kal, Lucas, and Mason bring personality and a whole lot of flavor to every single video.
